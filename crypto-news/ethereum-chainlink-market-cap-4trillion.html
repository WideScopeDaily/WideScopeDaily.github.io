Ethereum & Chainlink Surge as Crypto Market Blasts Past $4 Trillion

Share on X Share on Facebook Share on WhatsApp

The cryptocurrency market has smashed through the $4 trillion barrier this week, propelled by a robust surge in altcoins led by Ethereum and Chainlink. Ethereum climbed more than 21% to levels last seen in late 2021, while Chainlink stole the spotlight with an impressive 33.6% rally.

This shift in momentum saw Bitcoin's market dominance dip from 62% to about 57%, reflecting a growing appetite for decentralized finance projects and utility-focused tokens. Notable gainers included Dogecoin (+24%), Stellar (XLM) (+20%), and speculative assets such as MYX, which soared 1,400%, along with TROLL (+273%) and SOON (+159%).
